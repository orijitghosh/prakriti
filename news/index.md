# Changelog

## prakriti 0.1.0

Initial release.

- 30 palettes covering sequential, diverging, and qualitative types,
  inspired by India’s natural landscapes.
- [`prakriti_palette()`](https://orijitghosh.github.io/prakriti/reference/prakriti_palette.md)
  for raw hex vectors with discrete or continuous output.
- [`scale_color_prakriti()`](https://orijitghosh.github.io/prakriti/reference/scale_prakriti.md)
  /
  [`scale_colour_prakriti()`](https://orijitghosh.github.io/prakriti/reference/scale_prakriti.md)
  /
  [`scale_fill_prakriti()`](https://orijitghosh.github.io/prakriti/reference/scale_prakriti.md)
  for `ggplot2`.
- [`display_prakriti()`](https://orijitghosh.github.io/prakriti/reference/display_prakriti.md)
  for base-graphics palette inspection.
- [`prakriti_names()`](https://orijitghosh.github.io/prakriti/reference/prakriti_names.md),
  [`prakriti_info()`](https://orijitghosh.github.io/prakriti/reference/prakriti_info.md)
  for discovery.
