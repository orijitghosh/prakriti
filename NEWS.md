# prakriti 0.1.0

Initial release.

* 30 palettes covering sequential, diverging, and qualitative types,
  inspired by India's natural landscapes.
* `prakriti_palette()` for raw hex vectors with discrete or continuous output.
* `scale_color_prakriti()` / `scale_colour_prakriti()` / `scale_fill_prakriti()`
  for `ggplot2`.
* `display_prakriti()` for base-graphics palette inspection.
* `prakriti_names()`, `prakriti_info()` for discovery.
